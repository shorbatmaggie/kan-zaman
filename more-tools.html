Shorba Tools

Shorba is a suite of visualization and analysis apps for researchers. In addition to Naẓar, Shorba includes the following:

  • Minhāj: [Coming Soon] a searchable database of digitized Arabic texts. Browse texts or find what you're looking for using utilities like exact match, fuzzy search, normalized queries that account for orthographic variation, root and lemma search, and clitic-aware search (e.g. search results for "كتاب" will also include words like كتاب، الكتاب، بالكتاب، كتابهن).
    • Nasab: a tool for visualizing the textual genealogies of legal commentaries from the Shāfiʿī madhhab. Explore the lineage of a single text or compare multiple texts at once.
